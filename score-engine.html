WhatScore Engine (Scorecraft) creates ORIGINAL music cues for your videos: cue sheet → palette → seeded MIDI candidates → sketch preview → REAPER/Ableton handoff → approved export into the video package. AI plans structure (optional); a deterministic composer writes the notes; you approve everything.
