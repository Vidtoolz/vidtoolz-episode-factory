# Demo List

Planning scope only. These rows define intended demos for "Avoid Bad AI Video Ideas Before You Script". They are not captured evidence and do not claim that recording has happened.

| demo | what it proves | setup needed | status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Start with a raw AI-generated idea list and ask whether each idea gives the viewer a concrete post-video action. | Demonstrates that idea volume is not the same as strategy. | Prepare a local list of candidate ideas with one intentionally broad example. | planned |
| Score the rejected broad idea against the package criteria. | Shows why a topical idea can fail when payoff, proof, or thumbnail promise is vague. | Prepare scorecard rows for title clarity, thumbnail promise, viewer pain, payoff, proof, difficulty, and risk. | planned |
| Score the selected idea "Avoid Bad AI Video Ideas Before You Script" against the same criteria. | Shows why the selected package is stronger and production-feasible. | Prepare the selected package artifact and matching scorecard view. | planned |
| Compare rejected and selected ideas side by side. | Makes the human decision process visible instead of implied. | Prepare a comparison board with rejected idea, selected idea, criteria, and short reasons. | planned |
| Apply the veto question: "what will the viewer be able to do differently after watching?" | Gives viewers a repeatable filter they can use before scripting. | Prepare a short text card or highlighted script excerpt for the veto rule. | planned |
| Show the proof-object requirement: raw suggestions, scorecard, and final human-selected package must all be visible. | Explains why the video can be demonstrated rather than only narrated. | Prepare local windows or Resolve placeholders for the three-part proof object. | planned |
| Show a packaging check where title and thumbnail direction promise the same practical action. | Connects strategy filtering to packaging without claiming final title or thumbnail lock. | Prepare draft title and thumbnail-direction cards from run artifacts. | planned |
