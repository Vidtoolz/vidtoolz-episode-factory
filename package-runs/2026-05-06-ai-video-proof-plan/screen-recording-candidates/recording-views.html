Better Role of AI

AI handles expandable work. Mikko handles accountable work.

AI mayPrepare, inspect, draft, compare, expose gaps

Good targets: research scanning, options, formatting, logistics, checklists, pickup planning, metadata, mechanical review.

AI must notApprove itself or imply proof that does not exist

No final topic choice, final voice, proof sufficiency, publish approval, or readiness decisions.

Mikko mustJudge claim, proof, taste, lived experience, final publishability

The human role is not just operating the camera. It is judgment.

Practical workflowChoose → Prove → Produce → Review → Package → Publish

Slow down only where friction protects the work.
