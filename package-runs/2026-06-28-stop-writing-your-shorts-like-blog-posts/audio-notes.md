# Audio Notes

## Mic / Capture Notes

- Use the normal VIDTOOLZ mic setup.
- Record clean A-roll or voiceover from final-script.md only.
- Capture a short room tone or silence sample.
- Retake any line that sounds read, rushed, or unclear.

## Voiceover Notes

- Keep delivery conversational.
- The script should sound like a person talking, not an essay being read.
- Do not add explanatory lines that are not in final-script.md unless Mikko separately approves a script revision.

## Music / SFX Notes

- Music, if used, stays below narration.
- Use simple emphasis only where it supports the rewrite/contrast beats.

## Risks

- The script is short, so weak delivery or slow pacing will make the idea feel thin.
- Audio must stay clear because the core lesson is that Shorts are for listening.
