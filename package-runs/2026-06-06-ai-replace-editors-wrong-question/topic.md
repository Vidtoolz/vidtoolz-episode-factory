# Topic

## Working area

Why "AI Will Replace Editors" Is the Wrong Question

## One-paragraph topic

"AI will replace editors" is the wrong framing, and it's been the wrong framing through every tool transition in editing history — from razor blades to Avid to NLEs to AI. The real question is not whether AI can cut (it can, badly) but whether an editor can learn to use AI the way they learned to use every new tool before it. This video makes one concrete claim: editing isn't shrinking into automation — it's expanding beyond timeline work. The editors who thrive won't be the ones who compete with AI. They'll be the ones who learn to direct it. Told from the perspective of a working editor who's been through enough tech transitions to know the pattern.
