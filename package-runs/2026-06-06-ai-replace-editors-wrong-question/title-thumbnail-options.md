## Working Title Options

### Option A: Why "AI Will Replace Editors" Is the Wrong Question
- **Style:** Direct, question-reframing
- **Fit:** Strong — this is the stated topic and the thesis of the script
- **Risk:** Slightly long for a thumbnail
- **YouTube fit:** Good. The "why X is wrong" format performs.

### Option B: AI Won't Replace Editors. Here's What Actually Happens.
- **Style:** Assertion + curiosity gap
- **Fit:** Very strong — makes a claim, promises an answer
- **Risk:** None obvious
- **YouTube fit:** Excellent. Claim + gap = click.

### Option C: I'm an Editor. Here's What AI Actually Means for Us.
- **Style:** Personal, insider perspective
- **Fit:** Strong for VIDTOOLZ — Mikko's personal authority as working editor
- **Risk:** Less specific than B
- **YouTube fit:** Good. Insider/confessional angle.

### Option D: Stop Worrying About AI — The Editor's Job Is Expanding, Not Shrinking
- **Style:** Reassuring, counter-narrative
- **Fit:** Good — captures the core reframe
- **Risk:** "Stop worrying" can sound dismissive
- **YouTube fit:** Moderate.

### Hermes recommendation: Option B
- Strongest balance of claim + curiosity + clarity.
- Option C is the best personal-authority angle if Mikko wants to lean into his professional identity.

## Thumbnail Concepts

### Concept 1: The Editor Is Fine
- **Visual:** Mikko, relaxed, slight smirk, arms crossed, on green screen
- **Background:** AI robot hand reaching for a timeline, but Mikko's real hand is already there
- **Text overlay:** "NOT REPLACED" or "STILL HERE"
- **Vibe:** Confident, warm, slightly amused

### Concept 2: Wrong Question
- **Visual:** Mikko looking directly at camera, one eyebrow raised
- **Background:** Split — left half "AI REPLACE EDITORS?" (angry red), right half calm editor workspace (warm)
- **Text overlay:** "WRONG QUESTION"
- **Vibe:** Direct, correcting, authoritative

### Concept 3: The Pattern
- **Visual:** Mikko mid-gesture, explaining
- **Background:** Timeline showing four transitions: Razor → Avid → NLE → AI — all in a row, editors still standing at the end
- **Text overlay:** "THEY SAID THIS BEFORE"
- **Vibe:** Seen-it-all wisdom, reassuring

### Concept 4: Direct, Don't Compete
- **Visual:** Mikko at edit desk from behind, looking at timeline
- **Background:** Faint AI tool icons floating, all pointed AT the timeline — Mikko is in front of them
- **Text overlay:** "DIRECT IT"
- **Vibe:** In control, professional, forward-looking
