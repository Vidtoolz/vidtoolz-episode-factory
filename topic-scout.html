VIDTOOLZ Episode Factory — review 25 system-generated candidates and submit your own topic ideas for system review.
Run: 2026-06-24-ideation · Format: YouTube Short, max 3 min spoken word (~390-450 words)

How to use this page
Step 1 — Review candidates: The left column shows 25 system-generated topic candidates. Each card has the title, core idea, viewer promise, target viewer, production difficulty, and risk assessment.

Step 2 — Submit your own topic: Use the form in the right column to type a topic idea. The system will review it against 7 criteria: AI/creator relevance, professional authority fit, visual potential, specificity, one-claim pattern, trust risk, and overlap with abandoned topics.

Step 3 — Read the review: Each submitted topic gets an instant structured review with pass/fail per criterion and an overall recommendation. The review is an assessment, not an approval — you decide what enters the pipeline.

Step 4 — Select a topic: Once you've decided on a topic (from the candidates or your own submissions), go to the Package Engine to formally select it and start the production workflow.

Brand pattern reminder: One claim, one example, one point. Warm, personal, "told to a friend." Show craft, don't explain frameworks.
